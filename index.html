HemoSave

The HemoSave device reduces human error in estimating blood loss during C-section deliveries in low-resource hospitals. Designed for use in the operating room, it suctions blood and amniotic fluid into a vacuum container and collects remaining fluid with sponges. HemoSave then analyzes the fluids and reports the volume of blood lost, in milliliters every minute.

HemoSave stands out for its seamless integration into existing clinical workflows, its ability to distinguish blood from amniotic fluid, its sustainability as a solution, and its rapid, reliable updates to the care team.

About Us

Baini Technologies Limited is a team of engineers from Makerere University and Duke University. The HemoSave project began in the summer of 2023 in an effort to reduce postpartum morbidity through engineering. Our team grew throughout 2024, and, in 2025, Baini Technologies was founded to catapault the HemoSave device to greater heights.
